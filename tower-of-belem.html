Tower of Belém

»

The Tower of Belém was built between 1515 and 1520 as a monument to the Age of Discovery. It is built at the same location from which caravels departed during the height of Portugal's power at sea.

The architecture of this tower blends Gothic and Moorish elements, including ornate patterns of twisted rope carved from stone.
